Our Heritage

From the Atlas Mountains to London

My Moroccan Skin was born out of a desire to strip away the noise of modern cosmetics and return to purposeful, tactile skincare practices. Growing up with the generational secrets of the Moroccan Hammam, our founder sought to combine these restorative rituals with London’s standards for clean, sustainable cosmetic chemistry.

Every bottle reflects deep respect for architectural minimalism, premium transparency, and the raw power of unrefined botanical lipids.
